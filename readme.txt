Add Cooksnaps to your Blog and see what people cook with your recipes!

== Description ==

Cooksnaps is a service for receiving and organizing photos when people
cook your recipes, and creating deep conversations around them

= Cooksnaps for WordPress =
* Uses the Cooksnaps API
* Optionally activate for all pre-existing posts
* Can be easily shown or hidden per post
* Simple installation
* Can be positioned under your recipes or in the sidebar

= Cooksnaps Features =
* Users can upload a photo and message when they cook your recipe
* Notifications by email
* Reply and start conversations around each Cooksnap
* Cross links to other recipes on your site with cooksnaps
* Engage more deeply with your fans and followers
* View Recipe Analytics in our Simple Dashboard

== Installation ==

**NOTE: It is recommended that you [backup your database](http://codex.wordpress.org/Backing_Up_Your_Database) before installing the plugin.**

1. Search for "cooksnaps" in your WP Admin to install, or to manually install, unpack archive to this archive to the 'wp-content/plugins/' directory inside of WordPress

2. Register at [http://cooksnaps.com](http://cooksnaps.com) to get your
   unique Author Key

3. From your blog administration, click on 'Cooksnaps' to add your Author Key to WordPress

4. To optionally add Cooksnaps or the Cooksnaps Gallery as a sidebar widget, navigate to Appearance > Widgets in your WP Admin and drag from the available widgets list to the desired widget area.

= More documentation =

Go to [http://cooksnaps.com/widget#wordpress](http://cooksnaps.com/widget#wordpress)
